NEXORA is a neon-soaked arcade game where you pilot a UFO through a field of cyber columns. Dodge obstacles, rack up points, and challenge your high score in a retro-futuristic synthwave world.

Features: 
Smooth physics-based UFO controls
Dynamic difficulty with speed ramping
Neon synthwave visuals & particle effects
Persistent high score tracking
Desktop & mobile friendly

Controls: 
Desktop: Press SPACEBAR to jump
Mobile/Touch: Tap the screen

How to Play: 
Click INITIATE or press SPACEBAR to start
Navigate the UFO through the gaps in cyber columns
Avoid crashing and beat your best score

Tech Stack: 
HTML5 Canvas
Vanilla JavaScript
CSS3 (neon & particle effects)
